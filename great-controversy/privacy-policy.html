Privacy Policy for Intambara Ikomeye

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

Intambara Ikomeye ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the application.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data

We store the following data locally on your device using the Hive database:

  • Reading progress and bookmarks
  • Reading statistics
  • App preferences (theme, font settings)

Note: This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. To provide and optimize ads, AdMob may collect:

  • Unique device identifiers (e.g., AdID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (clicks/views)
  • App usage data related to ads

For more details, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • Reading Data: To provide a personalized reading experience and save your place in the text.
  • Ad Data: To show relevant advertisements and generate the revenue required to maintain the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information. All advertising data is collected directly and processed by Google AdMob according to their specific privacy protocols.

4. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data within the app:

  • Clear Data: You can wipe all reading history via Settings > Data.
  • Ad Opt-out: You can opt-out of personalized ads in your mobile device's system settings.
  • Total Removal: Deleting the app will automatically remove all local Hive data.

5. Children's Privacy

Intambara Ikomeye does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted directly within the app's interface.
